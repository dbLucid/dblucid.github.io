Privacy Policy

Ultimate Display Manager

Ultimate Display Manager does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything the app stores stays on your computer and is never sent anywhere.

What data does the app store?

Ultimate Display Manager saves configuration files locally on your device so it can remember your display profiles, settings, and preferences between sessions. These files are stored in your user profile directory and are never transmitted off your device.

The types of locally stored data include display profiles (monitor positions, resolutions, and layout preferences), application settings (hotkeys, theme choices, notification preferences), and a local cache of display hardware information used to manage monitors that are currently disconnected.

What data is collected or transmitted?

None. The app does not collect analytics, crash reports, telemetry, usage statistics, or any other data. It does not contact any server, and it does not transmit any information over the internet.

Third-party services

Ultimate Display Manager does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

The app is distributed through the Microsoft Store. Microsoft's own privacy practices for the Store are covered by the Microsoft Privacy Statement.

Children's privacy

The app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to this policy

If the app ever adds features that handle personal data, this policy will be updated before those features are released.
